If you're seeing this, it means that the next page is not yet out.

Niki Johnson and Hamish Upton's 'Navigating Distance and Closeness' will be released in 3 parts on a weekly basis.

This means:

  • Part 1 featuring the welcome and developing the instruments pages will be released on August 20th,
  • Part 2 with a description of the score and compositional process of the piece will be released on August 27th,
  • Part 3, the final part featuring a full video of their performance, will be released on September 3rd.

The good news is that the first section has already been released and can be found here.

If you have already enjoyed that part, you can find out more about Niki Johnson and Hamish Upton at their artist pages, or at their websites here for Niki, and here for Hamish.
