FABLE ARTS

Reimagined Storytelling

Experimental Art and Interdisciplinary Performance

Our events are designed to celebrate interdisciplinary storytelling.
By creating a community of musicians, artists, writers, filmmakers, scientists, and inventors (and more), we can engage with our shared past, our present, and look at what can be.
FABLE ARTS' aim is to expand what it means to experience art.
We curate concerts, exhibitions, and experiences, welcoming all audiences. With open doors (and ears), come along and share these stories with us.

OUR COMMITMENT STATMENT

FABLE ARTS is committed to working towards a sustainable future for contemporary art, music, and public programming.

To achieve this, our objectives are:

  1. Equal opportunity in all aspects of our practice inclusve of
    • artist representation,
    • consultation,
    • programming,
    • and curation;
  2. Challenge the limitations of traditional classical music and fine art, and to perform and commission 21st century music;
  3. Promote healthy discourse and encourage open critique of our practices;
  4. Champion the right to experience and access art for all, by actively improving our open access and accessibility practices - digitally and physically - and seek to engage with broader audiences internationally.

In addition, ALL our artists, programs, and opportunities are PAID.
We do not support the exploitative culture where artists, particularly young and emerging, are expectecd to work unpaid in exchange for exposure.
