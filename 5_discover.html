FABLE ARTS Book Club

Did you know that we have a book club?

Sign up throuh our contact page. Just put FA Book Club as the subject and let us know what you're interested in reading.

We will put forward a new title to read 3 times a year. Our favourite books are those from our artists, or those which talk about how to use art to improve the human condition. After reading, we have a discussion available to those who sign up through our Discord

We'd also love to hear your suggestions, either on the Discord, or through our contact page.
