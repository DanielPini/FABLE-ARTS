FABLE ARTS Book Club

Did you know that we have a book club?


Sign up throuh our contact page. Just put FA Book Club as the subject and let us know what you're interested in reading.


Each year we suggest a short-list of books from which the community can vote. The 3 winning titles then are released for the book club from February to June, July to October, and November to January.

Our favourite books are those from our artists, or those which talk about how to use art to improve the human condition. After reading, we have a discussion available to those who sign up through our Discord


We'd also love to hear your suggestions, either on the Discord, or through our contact page.
